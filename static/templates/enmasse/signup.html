Above template shows blockDateSTartEndTime with two blocks.
minTime ranges: [5,30] in iterations of 5
maxTime ranges: [minTime,minTime*6]
Preference duration needs to be between 10 and 60 minutes, in iterations of 10.
Fields maxSlotsPerUser and minTime and maxTime are irrelevant if preference based
